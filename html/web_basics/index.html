How To Cook the PERFECT Indoor Steak

In certain climates, the outdoor grill is just not always an option. We can not let this resort us to mere vegetables. Here you will learn how to cook the perfect steak while staying warm and dry.

Requirements

  • Cast Iron Skillet -

  • Steak -

  • Salt & Pepper -

Instructions

  1. Season steak generously with salt & pepper
  2. Pat steak until dry
  3. Heat Cast Iron Skillet until smoking
  4. Place steak into pan and DO NOT TOUCH FOR 3 MINUTES
  5. Flip and do not touch for 3 minutes
  6. Insert into 400 degree oven until internal temp reaches 125
  7. DEVOUR
